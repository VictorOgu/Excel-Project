Davinches LTD Annual Sales Analysis

Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Davinches LTD’s annual performance, focusing on revenue trends, 
top-performing sales representatives, product contributions, and geographic performance. The goal is to uncover 
actionable business insights and highlight patterns that can guide strategic decision-making.

Key Highlights

Total revenue closed at $435k, with a peak in December ($66,642.78), indicating strong year-end performance.
Top sales contributors (Nancy and Anne Larsen) drive a significant portion of revenue, showing high individual impact.
The Beverages product category leads revenue generation, followed by Sauces and Jams & Preserves.
Geographic analysis shows the North region as the dominant market, with New York being the top-performing city.
Findings reveal concentration in top performers, key products, and major clients, providing areas of focus for
sustainable growth.
